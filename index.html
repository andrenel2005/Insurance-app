Overview of Funeral Insurance

Funeral insurance provides a payout upon the insuredâ€™s death to cover funeral costs. Sanlam offers family-oriented coverage with various options to meet clients' needs.

Coverage typically ranges from R10,000 to R100,000, with add-ons like doubling the coverage after three years under specific plans.

Key Features of Sanlam Funeral Cover

Premiums start from R25 per month, allowing clients to cover up to 20 family members, including extended family, subject to South African residency.

Additional benefits include a wealth bonus, immediate accidental death coverage, and customizable plans.

Policy Structure and Documentation

Policies include waiting periods (usually six months for natural death) and may exclude certain causes of death. Claims are typically processed swiftly once documentation is submitted.

Clients can nominate beneficiaries to ensure payout distribution as per their wishes.
